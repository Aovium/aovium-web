Who We Are

Why Aovium

Aovium is a veteran-owned technology firm built on a straightforward principle: federal agencies deserve software partners who understand both the technical demands and the accountability frameworks that govern public-sector work.

Our team brings hands-on experience in secure application development, enterprise systems integration, and the compliance landscape surrounding federal IT. We build software that ships on schedule, meets authority-to-operate requirements, and serves the mission long after deployment.

Based in Washington, we work with agencies and prime contractors who value clear communication, transparent delivery, and engineering discipline over sales theater.
